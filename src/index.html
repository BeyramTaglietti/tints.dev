What's the idea here?

        

          Start with a base colour. This is the middle swatch. Tweaks to the
          Hue and Saturation will increasly
          change at either end. Control how light and dark the extremes are with
          the Min and Max values.
        

        

          This is by no means perfect, but it's a handy tool to get started.
